Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data TikTok Library App ("App") processes and how it is handled.

1. Data We Process

  • Account integration data (OAuth token references and related metadata).
  • Channel and video metadata (handles, IDs, captions, publish time, links, thumbnails, duration, raw API metadata).
  • Operational data (job status, retry state, event logs, settings).
  • Files saved by user configuration (downloaded media and sidecar JSON metadata).

2. How We Use Data

  • Authenticate account access where enabled by the user.
  • Detect and process new uploads for monitored channels.
  • Run download, retry, and queue workflows.
  • Provide dashboard visibility, status, and diagnostics.

3. Storage and Security

  • Data is stored locally by default.
  • Secret values are intended to be stored via local secure keychain mechanisms where available.
  • No guarantee of absolute security; users remain responsible for local system hardening and access controls.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not sell personal data. Data may be transmitted to third-party services only as required to operate configured integrations (for example TikTok APIs and downloader requests).

5. Retention

Data and downloaded files are retained until deleted by the user. Users control retention policy in local operation.

6. Your Choices

  • You can disconnect integrations and remove local data at any time.
  • You can stop monitoring channels or disable features in settings.

7. Children

The App is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. The effective date at the top indicates the latest version.

9. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, open an issue at GitHub Issues.
